What is The Financial Wellness Evaluation Tool?

This tool gives employers the power to help their employees assess and support their complete financial picture, and ultimately increase focus, productivity, and happiness in the workplace.

Financial Stress in the Workplace

It’s no surprise that many Americans struggle to achieve financial wellness in their lives. This often leads to mental and emotional fatigue, and financial paralysis. Many people are lost and don’t know where to go or what to do. Learn how financial instability to lower productivity in the work place

Our Approach

The Financial Wellness Evaluation Tool helps employers understand the complete picture of their staff’s financial well-being, and gives them simple, actionable steps to support them.

Understand Data

The Financial Wellness Evaluation Tool sends a quick and completely anonymous survey to an employer’s staff. Through a survey generated report, the employer will learn how their employees feel about their financial stress and goals.

Take Action

Employers can take action to support their staff using the curated list of city services that the evaluation tool has recommended.
