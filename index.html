Welcome to your new Financial Wellness Tool.

This tools will ask you some questions about your personal finances and then provide you with a list of resources like connecting you to social and non-profit services, coaching and workshops, and information to help you better understand your financial situation. Overall we seek to help you and your employer understand how to best support you financially as an employee. This support will include low interest loans and matched saving account which can help you manage your day to day figures. Keep in mind that although the cumulative information will be shared with your employer— your individual results will not.
